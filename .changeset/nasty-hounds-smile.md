Add person-card interaction support to `Person` with `personCardInteraction` options (`none`, `hover`, `click`).

Add a reusable `PersonCard` component for rendering person contact details and quick contact actions.

When enabled, `Person` now shows a hover/click details popover with additional profile details and contact actions (email, Teams chat, call), plus fetches the extra profile fields needed for that card.
